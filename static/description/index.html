Local Video Snippet

Step 1

Drag any structural component onto the page

Step 2

Drag and drop video components into this structural component

Step 3

Next, you can double-click the video component or click the edit button on the right to bring up the settings pop-up window

Step 4

Click the button to upload the local video. Currently, only MP4 format videos are supported. After the video is uploaded, the link address will be automatically filled in the input box. Also, if you know the exact parameters, you can directly modify them in the input box

Step 5

You can make parameter adjustments to the video player

Step 6

After saving, you will see that the video has been successfully added to the page. At this time, you can adjust the video size in the right panel
